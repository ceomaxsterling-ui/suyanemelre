A professional and highly readable "Artigo do Blog" (Blog Post) template page for deep-dive financial content.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Theme: Light, Professional, Executive, "Clean Glassmorphism" for UI elements, "Utilitarian" for typography
- Background: Silver Light (#F8FAFC) for the outer page, Pure White (#FFFFFF) for the main reading area
- Primary Accent: Deep Navy (#1E293B) for primary buttons, impactful headings, and authority elements
- Secondary Accent: Executive Grey (#475569) for subtitles, dates, and body text
- Surface: Pure White (#FFFFFF) with Metallic Silver (#E2E8F0) borders for elevated containers
- Buttons: Pill-shaped (rounded-full)
- Cards & Reading Area: Generously rounded corners (rounded-3xl), subtle drop shadows (shadow-xl)
- Typography (Headers): Lexend (tracking-tight)
- Typography (Body): Inter (larger size for comfortable reading, e.g., text-lg with relaxed line-height)
- Layout Strategy: Narrower reading column for optimal readability (max-w-3xl inside a max-w-7xl container). Generous whitespace.

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header:** Same navigation bar as the home page with logo and menu items
2. **Post Header (Hero):** Centered title "O impacto da Selic no seu portfólio de longo prazo", with metadata below: Author (Suyane Melre), Date (14 de Dezembro, 2023), and Category (Macroeconomia) tag.
3. **Featured Image:** A large aspect-video (16:9) rounded-3xl grayscale image below the title, serving as the cover.
4. **Reading Area (Main Content):** A clean Pure White (#FFFFFF) column containing dummy article content with rich formatting: an introduction, a quotation block (blockquote), an H2 subheading, and a bulleted list. Text should be Executive Grey (#475569) for low eye strain.
5. **Share & Author Block:** A section at the bottom of the article with share icons (LinkedIn, Email) and a small author bio card (image of Suyane + short description).
6. **Related Articles:** A "Leia Também" section at the bottom with a 2-column or 3-column grid of smaller article cards (similar to the home page or blog list).
7. **Footer:** Same standard footer as the home page
