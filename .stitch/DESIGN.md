# Design System: Suyane Melre CFP®
**Project ID:** 14610748245177721276

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
Aesthetic is Highly Professional, Executive, and Trustworthy. Clean minimal backgrounds with "glassmorphism" components. The atmosphere speaks to high-net-worth individuals: sophisticated, secure, and structured. Grayscale images with hover effects reveal color to create a dynamic but restrained corporate feel.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Deep Navy (#1E293B):** Used for primary text, bold impactful headings, and primary call-to-action buttons. Conveys authority and depth.
- **Executive Grey (#475569):** Used for secondary text, descriptions, and subtitles. Conveys balance and mature professionalism.
- **Metallic Silver (#E2E8F0):** Used for borders, subtle dividers, and progress/chart backgrounds.
- **Silver Light (#F8FAFC):** The primary off-white page background. Eliminates harsh contrast while keeping the page airy.
- **Pure White (#FFFFFF):** Used for floating cards and elevated containers to make them pop against the Silver Light background.

## 3. Typography Rules
- **Headers:** `Lexend` (Google Font), used across h1 to h4. Weights from 400 to 700. Often tight tracking (`tracking-tight`) for a modern, sleek look.
- **Body & Accents:** `Inter` (Google Font), used for readable paragraph text and UI accents (like uppercase tags).

## 4. Component Stylings
- **Buttons:** Pill-shaped (`rounded-full`). Primary buttons are Deep Navy with white text text, slightly growing or gaining shadow on hover.
- **Cards/Containers:** Generously rounded corners (`rounded-2xl` or `rounded-3xl` or `1.5rem` to `2.5rem`). Background is usually Pure White with thin Metallic Silver borders (`border border-silver`). Often uses slight drop shadows (`shadow-xl`).
- **Tags/Eyebrows:** Very small uppercase text (`text-[10px]` or `text-xs`) with wide tracking (`tracking-widest`), often wrapped in a subtle border.
- **Icons:** Minimalist Material Symbols Outlined, typically Deep Navy or Silver.

## 5. Layout Principles
- Huge amounts of whitespace to breathe (`py-20` to `py-24` between sections).
- Max-width containers (`max-w-7xl`).
- Grid structures for services and articles (1-col on mobile, 3 or 4-col on desktop).
- Interlocking structural elements (like slightly overlapping images or side-by-side text/image layouts).
